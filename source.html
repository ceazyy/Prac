About

Creatively independent since 1986

We don’t tell stories. We don’t make multi-slided decks. We don’t rake in followers. We don’t scrounge for likes. We don’t purport to save the world.

In all likelihood, we’re probably not the right agency for you. In fact, we don’t even think of ourselves as an agency. We think of ourselves as an independent creative lab.

We don’t just create ad campaigns, instead we stir things up. We experiment with ideas to come up with the best solution to your business problem.

Our methods might be a bit unexpected, a bit scary, and might make you a bit uncomfortable, but you can be gosh-darned certain they will be effective.

Independent. Integrated. And Indian.

There are integrated agencies, but they aren’t independent. There are independent agencies, but they aren’t integrated. And if they are both independent and integrated, they aren’t Indian.

Established in 1986, Crayons is one of the few agencies that can truly claim to be a fully integrated, fully independent, and a through and through Indian agency.

Being independent means, the buck stops with us. So, there is no beating around corporate bushes, you deal directly with the people who work on your business.

Being integrated means, we offer world class creative solutions that solve our clients’ business problems.

And being Indian means, we understand local trends far better than any agency headquartered in Madison Avenue.

What’s more, the fact that we have 9 offices across various regions, means we are well-versed in regional nuances. And speak to people in the language that they think in.

This Is Us

The 225 people who today form Team Crayons, are just the right mix of experience, madness, insights, disruption, skills, expertise, and passion. They’re just the sort of people who you can depend on to turn your business problem on its head or just have a long knowledgeable chat. The Team is led by equally interesting and competent people.

Our Team

  • Manoj Jacob Executive Creative Director

    Manoj has over 20 years of experience working across agencies like Ogilvy, DDB Mudra, Havas, BBDO, and Contract Advertising. He has helped build several brands such as Peter England, IBM, Lenovo, Himalaya Wellness, Louis Phillipe, Diageo, Exxon Mobil, GE, UNICEF, Fosters, Reckitt, etc. Manoj’s work has won him two Cannes Lions.

    Other commendable accolades won by him include an Adfest Lotus, One Show Merit, Media Spikes, and a Clio for ‘Best Writing in Print’.

    Manoj believes that you are only as good as your last idea.

  • Vijay Anand Associate Vice President, Chennai

    Vijay has worked across like MAA Bozell, Disha Communications and RK Swamy BBDO for more than 22 years. He has handled several brands like Hero Honda, Amrutanjan, COMFY Sanitary Napkins, etc. He was also instrumental in launching CBZ in Andhra Pradesh. Vijay is a staunch believer in the power of life, and values people and relationships.

  • Sanjeev Bhirani Senior Vice President, Mumbai

    A postgraduate in Marketing Management with a Diploma in Public Relation and Journalism, eMBA from MET, MUMBAI, Sanjeev has been trained at the National Training Institute, Muscat.

    He has 24 years industry experience and has handled prestigious accounts like British Bank of the Middle East, Toyota Passenger Cars, Qantas Airways, SANYO, CANON, Oman Cement, ANZ Grindlays Bank and Pantaloon Fashions.

    He has worked with Percept, Speer – FCB, ADINC- PUBLI GRAPHICS, United Media- Leo Middle East and Pressman Advertising.

  • Sunil Daftary Branch Director, Kolkata

    Sunil has 18 years of experience in the industry. He started his career at Crayons as an intern at the age of 19.   • Debanjali Sen Branch Director, Lucknow

    Debanjali is a seasoned growth, engagement, and marketing professional with an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad. Her career is marked by a blend of entrepreneurship and strategic expertise, having worked with leading agencies such as Ogilvy, McCann, Rediffusion, and Tag. She brings extensive experience from the production industry, where she has produced ad films featuring renowned celebrities like PV Sindhu, Ajay Devgan, the late Sushant Singh Rajput, Varun Dhawan and more. Debanjali has successfully spearheaded major brand launches, including Sobieski Vodka, Discovery Whisky, Richa Realty’s first urban project, and the first-ever studio apartments for Siddha Realty. Known for her innovative approach and results-driven mindset, she believes in lifelong learning and is a passionate shooter.

  • Kunal Lalani Founder and Chairman

    Kunal, single-handedly set up Crayons in1986. Today, his in-depth understanding of the intricacies of Indian businesses provides strategic direction and business solutions to all verticals.

  • Ashraye Lalani Director, Growth & Technology

    Ashraye graduated from City University of London, where he pursued an honours degree in a Bachelor’s of Science in Economics with Accounting. After this, he also acquired a Bachelor’s of Science Degree in strategic management from The London School Of Economics. Ashraye brings a global perspective and an understanding of international standards of consumerism, which forges a key pillar in Crayons’ efforts to provide cutting-edge digital offerings and plays poetically into the agency’s ambition to pursue its global expansion. At Crayons, Ashraye has played a crucial role in driving major new business and has been a key part in securing the majority of vital clients heralded in Crayons’ portfolio today. He heads the growth and technology wing at Crayons, and has conceptualised and delivered digital & tech-integrated campaigns for clients.

  • Ranjan Bargotra Executive Director

    With over 35 years of leadership in brand development, business operations, and talent management, Ranjan is an adman through and through. As President of Crayons, he didn’t just run the agency, he scaled it into India’s largest independent agency. Along the way, he played a pivotal role in shaping brands like Kajaria Tiles, Fortis Healthcare, Kohinoor Rice, ITC Fortune Hotels, and APL Apollo. Beyond brands, he has steered over two dozen election campaigns for BJP, Congress, and regional parties, working closely with some of India’s most influential leaders. But Ranjan isn’t just about brands and ballots, he’s about people. His ability to connect across ideologies and generations makes him a natural fit for the board of Abhinandan, a digital health start-up, where he brings strategic communication and creative oversight to help the brand speak, resonate, and lead.

  • Samir Datar Chief Strategy Officer

    Samir has 30 years of experience, working across agencies like JWT, Cheil, Grey, and Hakuhodo. He was responsible for the resurgence of Maggi Noodles in 1999, led the team that launched Nature Fresh, and for the launch and success of Samsung Mobile Phones in India.

    At Crayons, as CSO, Samir has won Air India and Croma in large multi-agency pitches. He has also been part of Swach Bharat initiatives with the government, and led the teams that created campaigns for Indian Army, Indian Air Force, and the Indian Navy.
