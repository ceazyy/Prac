01

Creatively Independent Since 1986

We don't tell stories. We don't make multi-slided decks. We don't rake in followers. We don't scrounge for likes. We don't purport to save the world.

In all likelihood, we're probably not the right agency for you. In fact, we don't even think of ourselves as an agency. We think of ourselves as an independent creative lab.

We don't just create ad campaigns, instead we stir things up. We experiment with ideas to come up with the best solution to your business problem.

02

Independent. Integrated. Indian.

There are integrated agencies, but they aren't independent. There are independent agencies, but they aren't integrated. And if they are both independent and integrated, they aren't Indian.

Established in 1986, Crayons is one of the few agencies that can truly claim to be a fully integrated, fully independent, and a through and through Indian agency.

Being independent means, the buck stops with us. Being integrated means, we offer world class creative solutions. And being Indian means, we understand local trends far better than any agency headquartered in Madison Avenue.

Leadership Team

The creative minds behind our success

Kunal Lalani

Founder and Chairman

Kunal, single-handedly set up Crayons in 1986. Today, his in-depth understanding of the intricacies of Indian businesses provides strategic direction and business solutions to all verticals.

Ranjan Bargotra

Executive Director

With over 35 years of leadership in brand development, business operations, and talent management, Ranjan is an adman through and through. As President of Crayons, he scaled it into India's largest independent agency.

Samir Datar

Chief Strategy Officer

Samir has 30 years of experience, working across agencies like JWT, Cheil, Grey, and Hakuhodo. He was responsible for the resurgence of Maggi Noodles in 1999, and led campaigns for Indian Army, Air Force, and Navy.

Ashraye Lalani

Director, Growth & Technology

Ashraye graduated from City University of London and The London School Of Economics. He brings a global perspective and heads the growth and technology wing at Crayons.

Manoj Jacob

Executive Creative Director

Manoj has over 20 years of experience working across agencies like Ogilvy, DDB Mudra, Havas, BBDO. He has helped build several brands and won two Cannes Lions along with other prestigious awards.

Vijay Anand

Branch Director

Vijay has experience spanning over 20 years with expertise in ATL, BTL, Activations, Events, and Digital Marketing across various categories and brands.
